## What Each Tool Actually Does

### Hootsuite

Hootsuite is a **social media management platform**. Its core jobs are:
- Schedule and publish posts across multiple social platforms
- Monitor brand mentions and keyword conversations (social listening)
- Track engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments, follower growth)
- Manage multi-account social media workflows for teams
- Generate performance reports for stakeholders

Hootsuite's model is breadth: one platform to manage your entire social presence across Instagram, X, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest, and more.

It does the publishing-and-monitoring job well.

### Signal Harvester

Signal Harvester is an **Instagram lead generation tool**. Its core job is:
- Scan Instagram (and TikTok) comments in real time
- Detect buying intent signals (questions, frustrations, comparisons, triggers)
- Score comments by purchase likelihood
- Generate reply suggestions for high-intent prospects
- Surface the comments that represent the highest sales opportunities

Signal Harvester's model is depth: one specific thing, done exceptionally well — finding buyers hiding in comment sections.
