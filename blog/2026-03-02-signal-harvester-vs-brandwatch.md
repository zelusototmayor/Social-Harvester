## What Brandwatch Actually Does

Brandwatch is an **enterprise social intelligence platform**. Its primary job is to tell you what people are saying about your brand, your competitors, and your industry — across the entire internet.

The tool pulls from:
- Social media platforms (Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok, Reddit, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn)
- News sites and online publications
- Blogs and forums
- Review sites and podcasts

It then applies natural language processing to categorise that data by sentiment, topic, location, demographic, and reach — surfacing trends, tracking brand health over time, and alerting you to spikes in conversation volume.

**Brandwatch is designed to answer:**
- "What is the internet saying about my brand this week?"
- "How does my share of voice compare to competitors?"
- "Is sentiment improving or declining following our campaign?"
- "What topics are trending in my industry?"
- "Where did this PR crisis originate, and how far has it spread?"

It's a **monitoring and analytics** tool. The output is insight: dashboards, trend reports, sentiment graphs, audience demographics, competitive benchmarks.

**Who Brandwatch is built for:** Enterprise marketing teams, brand managers, PR agencies, and analysts who need to measure and understand social media at scale. Typical customers are teams with dedicated social media analysts and $1k+/month social media budgets.

## What Signal Harvester Actually Does

Signal Harvester is an **Instagram and TikTok lead generation tool**. Its primary job is to find people in comment sections who are actively showing buying intent — and surface them to you before the opportunity disappears.

The tool scans:

It then applies buying intent detection to identify the comments that signal a purchase decision in motion: someone asking for a product recommendation, complaining about a competitor, expressing a specific pain your product solves, tagging a friend who "needs to see this."

**Signal Harvester is designed to answer:**

It's a **prospecting and sales** tool. The output is action: a ranked queue of people to message, generated reply suggestions, and real-time alerts so you reach intent-driven prospects while their buying signal is still hot.

**Who Signal Harvester is built for:** Founders, DTC brands, agencies, coaches, and SaaS teams who want to generate leads from Instagram and TikTok comment sections. Customers range from solo operators to teams of five.
