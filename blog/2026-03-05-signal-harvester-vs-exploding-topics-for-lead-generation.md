Exploding Topics and Signal Harvester solve different stages of growth.

Exploding Topics is a market discovery engine. It helps you see themes, products, and categories that are gaining momentum.

Signal Harvester is a lead activation engine. It helps you identify people showing active buying intent in social conversations so your team can engage immediately.

## Fast comparison

- **Use Exploding Topics when:** you are deciding what to build, what niche to enter, or what content angles are rising.
- **Use Signal Harvester when:** you already know your ICP and need a daily stream of high-intent prospects.

## Workflow difference

### Exploding Topics workflow
1. Spot rising themes.
2. Validate with search/social.
3. Create campaigns around trends.
4. Wait for demand capture.

### Signal Harvester workflow
1. Define ICP and intent triggers.
2. Monitor comments/posts for buying language.
3. Score leads by urgency and fit.
4. Send contextual outreach while intent is fresh.

## Which tool creates pipeline faster?

For B2B outbound teams, Signal Harvester usually creates faster pipeline because it captures active demand. Trend tools are strategic; intent tools are operational.

## Ideal stack

Use both if possible:

- Exploding Topics for monthly strategic planning.
- Signal Harvester for daily lead generation execution.

That combination gives you long-term direction and short-term revenue motion.
